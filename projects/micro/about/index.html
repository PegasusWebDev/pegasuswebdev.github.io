Micro (WIP)

A simplistic web game using only geometric shapes.

Micro is a simplistic web game, inspired by A Dark Room by doublespeak games.

Explore a world that starts small. Like, microscopically small. And then it gets bigger. Interpret the strange world of shapes around you however you choose and discover all new mechanics as you progress.

Micro is still in development, keep an eye out for updates and patches!
